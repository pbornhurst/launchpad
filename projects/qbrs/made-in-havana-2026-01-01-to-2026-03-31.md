## Key Findings

1. **Strong growth trajectory with March as the standout month.** Q1 total sales were up +3.1% over Q4, but the real story is month-over-month: orders grew +22.5% and sales grew +33.0% from January to March. This indicates accelerating demand heading into Q2.

2. **Marketplace is the growth engine (+11.6% sales).** Marketplace drove 53% of Q1 revenue and grew nearly 12% over Q4. Menu conversion rose from 19.9% to 22.8% — a 3-point improvement that directly translates to more paying customers per menu view.

3. **Storefront is your highest-growth channel (+34% orders, +30% sales).** It's still small ($4,547 in Q1), but the trajectory is excellent and it carries no marketplace commission — every Storefront order has better margins than a Marketplace order.

4. **In-Store sales declined -10.3% despite orders growing slightly.** Order count was essentially flat (+1.1%) but AOV dropped from $24.08 to $21.36. Customers are ordering smaller baskets in-store — worth investigating whether seasonal mix, price changes, or promo mechanics are driving this.

5. **Your Marketplace discount rate of 13.5% is above target.** $4,894 in merchant-funded discounts on Marketplace sales is substantial — about 13.5% of gross Marketplace subtotal. The overall blended ROAS of 4.65x on promos is healthy but not excellent; Sponsored Listings at 9.17x ROAS is a significantly more efficient use of marketing dollars.

6. **Operations are excellent across the board.** Average Marketplace wait time of 2.0 minutes, cancellation rate of 0.63%, and error rate of 1.97% are all at or under target. Dashers are getting in and out quickly — this is a competitive advantage.

7. **Menu is heavily concentrated on bowls and sandwiches (69% of revenue).** Top 5 items alone account for 52% of all Q1 revenue. Mojo Chicken Bowl and The Cuban Sandwich tie for top honors at ~$9.7K each. Your bowl program is the clear winner — 11 SKUs delivering $30.7K.

8. **Repeat customer rate is strong (59% of online revenue).** With 423 repeat customers averaging 1.86 orders each, you have a durable base of returning fans.

## Recommendations

1. **Reallocate promotional spend toward Sponsored Listings.** SLs are delivering 9.17x ROAS versus 4.65x on merchant-funded promos. Consider raising SL daily budget by 25-50% for Q2 while dialing back on the Happy Hour discount (which runs at 4.82x ROAS). Expected impact: 2-3x more efficient new customer acquisition at similar spend.

2. **Audit the Cuban Sandwich category for SKU consolidation.** 18 SKUs are generating $18K in Q1 vs Bowls' 11 SKUs generating $31K. Multiple near-duplicate vegan and "Oh So Sweet" variants may be splitting customer choice. Consolidating to 10-12 clear winners could reduce menu clutter and lift conversion.

3. **Investigate the In-Store AOV decline.** A $2.72 drop in in-store AOV (from $24.08 to $21.36) is material. Review: (a) any recent menu price changes, (b) order pattern shifts (smaller orders per customer or fewer add-ons), (c) whether staff are still upselling sides and drinks at the counter.

4. **Drive Storefront growth with 1p-only incentives.** Storefront grew 34% but remains only 6% of sales. Consider a free side or dessert with orders over $25 via Storefront — this channel has no marketplace commission and costs less to scale.

5. **Improve photo coverage from 64% to 80%+.** 36% of your menu items lack photos, which directly correlates to lower conversion on those items. Based on observed weekly conversion lifts, closing this gap could add 2-3 points to overall conversion rate.

6. **Protect the repeat customer base.** With 423 customers already ordering 1.86x/quarter, a targeted DashPass Loyalty-style offer for 2x+ orderers (already running at 3.58 ROAS) should be scaled — these customers are lower-cost to retain than to acquire.

## Growth Opportunities

1. **Marketplace channel headroom.** Q1 Marketplace grew +11.6% with improving conversion. March alone ran $15,627 in Marketplace sales vs January's $9,614 (+63%). At this trajectory, full-quarter Q2 Marketplace could exceed $45K.

2. **Catering / Large Order potential via Storefront.** AOV on Storefront ($33.93) is already slightly higher than Marketplace ($33.98). With 134 orders in Q1, there's room to 2-3x this channel by surfacing group/family bundles (e.g., "Cuban Family Meal for 4") on the 1p site.

3. **Lunch hour optimization.** Mojo Chicken Bowl, The Cuban Sandwich, and The Cuban Bowl collectively drive $27K quarterly — these are prime weekday lunch items. Consider a weekday 11am-2pm Sponsored Listings daypart boost to capture office-worker demand.

4. **Vegan/GF segment expansion.** You already have a strong vegan portfolio (Vegan Cuban Sandwich, Vegan Bowl, Vegan Ropa Vieja Bowl) generating ~$4K in Q1 combined. Greater Los Angeles has strong vegan search demand — a targeted "100% Vegan Cuban" promo could attract net-new audiences.

5. **New customer pull-through.** 398 new customers acquired in Q1 at a blended $17.48 CPA. Historical data shows repeat customers order ~1.86x/quarter — capturing even 40% of these new customers into a second order could drive ~159 additional orders at $27 AOV = ~$4,300 incremental revenue.
