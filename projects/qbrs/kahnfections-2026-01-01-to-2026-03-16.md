## Key Findings

1. **Explosive In-Store Growth:** Your In-Store POS channel grew from 116 orders in the comparison period to 949 orders in the current period (+717%). This reflects strong adoption of the DoorDash POS system and consistent daily transaction volume averaging approximately 12-13 orders per day.

2. **Biscuit Sandwich is the Clear Hero Item:** Combined across menu categories, the Biscuit Sandwich drives approximately $107,141 in item-level revenue — nearly half (48.8%) of all product mix revenue. This signature item is the engine of your business.

3. **Strong Marketing Returns:** Your overall ROAS of 5.0x is healthy, with the "Spend $35 get $5 off" promotion delivering an exceptional 7.6x ROAS and the newer "Spend $30 get $5 off" campaign showing even stronger returns at 9.4x. The Loyalty Promo also performs well at 3.9x, primarily driving repeat engagement.

4. **Healthy Discount Rate:** Your overall discount rate of 2.1% is well within a sustainable range. In-Store discounts are minimal (1.0%), while Marketplace discounts (5.4%) are higher due to promotional campaigns, which is expected.

5. **Minimal Marketplace Presence:** Only 3 orders came through the Marketplace delivery/pickup channel during this 75-day period. This represents a significant untapped growth opportunity for reaching new customers who discover you on the DoorDash app.

6. **Sponsored Listings Showed Promise:** The Sponsored Listing campaign (Jan 14 – Feb 11) generated 176 orders at a 4.9x ROAS with 36 new customers. The campaign was paused after 29 days — restarting could drive meaningful incremental online orders.

7. **AOV Trending Upward:** Average Order Value improved from $10.90 in January to $12.60 in March (partial), a positive 15.6% trend within the period, suggesting customers may be adding more items per transaction.

---

## Recommendations

1. **Reactivate Sponsored Listings:** The campaign delivered a strong 4.9x ROAS before being paused in February. We recommend restarting at a similar budget level to rebuild Marketplace visibility and drive new customer acquisition. The 7.8% CTR indicates strong customer interest.

2. **Scale the "Spend $30/$35 get $5 off" Promotion Model:** These threshold-based promotions are your highest-performing campaigns (7.6x–9.4x ROAS). Consider keeping a version of this running continuously to incentivize higher basket sizes, especially for online orders.

3. **Grow Marketplace Channel:** With only 3 Marketplace orders this period, there is substantial room to grow delivery and pickup volume. This could be achieved by:
   - Reactivating Sponsored Listings to increase visibility
   - Ensuring your DoorDash menu is fully optimized with photos and descriptions
   - Setting competitive prep times to improve ranking in search results

4. **Monitor Biscuit Sandwich Quality on Marketplace:** The Breakfast Sandwiches category had 3 missing and 11 incorrect item reports. While rates are low relative to volume, ensuring consistent quality for your hero item — especially for delivery where you cannot fix errors in real time — will protect your ratings as Marketplace grows.

5. **Optimize the Loyalty Promo:** The Loyalty Promo drives the most orders (513) but has the lowest ROAS of your merchant-funded campaigns (3.9x). Consider adjusting the discount threshold or amount to improve returns while maintaining customer engagement.

6. **Reduce Open Item Transactions:** 151 "Open Item" entries suggest manual POS entries without menu item mapping. Working to capture these as named menu items will improve product mix reporting and help identify additional revenue opportunities.

7. **Explore Storefront (1st-Party Online Ordering):** Some Storefront transaction activity was detected. If not already active, enabling a fully branded online ordering experience through DoorDash Storefront could capture delivery-averse customers who prefer ordering directly.

## Growth Opportunities

1. **Marketplace Delivery Expansion:** Your business is almost entirely in-store today. Even a modest shift — capturing 5-10% of volume through Marketplace — could add 50-100 orders per month with higher AOVs (Marketplace AOV was $18.00 vs. In-Store $11.63). This represents potential incremental revenue of $900-$1,800/month.

2. **Catering Revenue:** Your menu includes catering items (Biscuit Sandwiches, Sweet Croissant Box, Verve Coffee Box) that have been ordered. With the Biscuit Sandwich as a proven crowd-pleaser, actively promoting catering for office breakfasts, events, and weekend gatherings could unlock a high-AOV revenue stream. A single catering order for "Biscuit Sandwiches (Biscuit, Spread, Cheese)" was $182.00.

3. **Upsell Beverage Attachment:** Coffee & Espresso Drinks represent 11.6% of revenue. Given that 55.2% of revenue comes from sandwiches/breakfast items, there may be an opportunity to increase beverage attachment rates through combo deals or suggestive selling at the POS.

4. **Weekend Marketing Push:** Sponsored Listing data showed Saturday/Sunday orders were consistently among the highest volume days (e.g., Jan 25 had 17 orders). Concentrating ad spend on weekends could maximize ROAS.

5. **New Customer Funnel:** Marketing campaigns acquired 203 new customers this period. Building a strategy to convert these first-time buyers into repeat customers (via the Loyalty Promo or targeted follow-up offers) could meaningfully grow the customer base.
