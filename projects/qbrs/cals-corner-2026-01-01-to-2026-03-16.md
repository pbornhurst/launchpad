## Key Findings

1. **Explosive Multi-Channel Growth:** Cal's Corner has grown from a Marketplace-only presence (1,152 orders in the comparison period) to a true omni-channel operation with 5,313 total orders across four channels — a 361% increase. Total sales grew 259% to $96,114.25. This transformation was driven by the launch of In-Store POS, Kiosk, and Storefront channels in February 2026.

2. **In-Store Channel Is the Volume Leader:** With 2,392 POS orders and 509 Kiosk orders (2,901 combined), In-Store has already surpassed Marketplace in order volume despite only being active since February. March is trending toward 2,800+ In-Store orders for the full month. Kiosk AOV ($14.14) outpaces POS ($11.29), suggesting customers ordering via Kiosk spend more per transaction.

3. **Marketplace Discount Rate Requires Attention (27.5%):** Marketplace discounting is extremely aggressive at 27.5% — with $14,130.56 in your discounts against $53,866.90 in subtotal. This is more than triple the 8% healthy benchmark and has increased significantly from 16.1% in the comparison period. While driving volume growth (+66% Marketplace orders), the margin impact is substantial.

4. **Strong Sponsored Listings Performance:** Both campaigns delivered healthy returns. The "Lapsed Customers" campaign achieved a 7.29x ROAS with a $3.65 CPA — excellent efficiency for re-engaging existing customers. The newer campaign is running at 5.75x ROAS while also acquiring 47 new customers. Overall click-to-order conversion of 33.5% is outstanding.

5. **Order Accuracy Needs Improvement (3.15% Error Rate):** While improved from 3.92% in the comparison period, the current 3.15% error rate still exceeds the 2% target. The highest-volume items — Brekkie Bagel - (2.0% issue rate) and Brekkie Burrito - (2.1% issue rate) — are contributing to this. Given these are the top revenue drivers, improving fulfillment accuracy on these two items alone would materially reduce the overall error rate.

6. **Storefront Showing Strong Early Traction:** Storefront launched with 76 orders in February and accelerated to 432 in the first 16 days of March — a 468% month-over-month increase.
